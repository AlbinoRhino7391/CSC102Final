Ajitsuke Tamago

Marinated Soft-Boiled Eggs

Needed to make Ichiraku Ramen or Use as a Snack

What you will need:

Ingredient Amount
Large Eggs 2
Chasu Marinade (see chasu pork recipe) 1/2 Cup

Directions:

  1. Fill a pot with enough water to cover the eggs. (*DO NOT PUT THE EGGS IN THE POT AT THIS TIME) Bring the water to a boil then reduce the heat to medium. Lower the eggs into the water and gently stir them around for 2 minutes, then let them cook for an additional 4 minutes. While the eggs are cooking, prepare an ice bath by filling a medium bowl with cold water and ice cubes. Once the eggs are cooked, quickly remove them and gently place them into the ice bath to cool.
  2. Once cool, gently tap and roll the eggs on the counter-top to crack the shells all over. Carefully peel away the shells, then place the eggs into a zip-lock bag. Pour the marinade over the eggs and seal the bag. Refrigerate over night. WHen ready to serve, remove the eggs from the bag and slice in half lenghtwise; the white will be solid and the yolks will be runny.
