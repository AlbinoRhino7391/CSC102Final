Menma

Pickled Bamboo Shoots

Needed to make Ichiraku Ramen

What you will need:

Ingredient Amount
Bamboo Shoot Strips 1 Cup
Chasu Marinade (see chasu pork recipe) 1/2 Cup
Mirin 1/2 Cup
Dashi Stock (See Recipe) 1 Cup

Directions:

  1. In a medium pot over medium heat, combine all the ingredients and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat to low, and let simmer for 30 minutes to soften the bamboo.
  2. Remove the pot from the heat and let the bamboo cool in the liquid. Pour in a sturdy zip-lock bag and Refrigerate overnight. Strain when ready to use.
